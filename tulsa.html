Why It's Special

Tulsa represents America's heartland with its unique blend of Art Deco architecture, Native American heritage, and modern attractions. The city's transformation from oil capital to cultural hub fascinates me.

How to Get There

  • Fly to Tulsa International Airport (TUL)
  • Rent a car for easy transportation
  • Local transport: buses and taxis available
  • Best time to visit: Spring (March-May) or Fall (September-November)

Must-Visit Places

  • Philbrook Museum of Art
  • Gathering Place Park
  • Route 66 Historical District
  • Woody Guthrie Center
  • Oklahoma Aquarium
  • Tulsa Zoo
